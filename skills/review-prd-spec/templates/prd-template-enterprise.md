## Notes

- All financial figures must be reviewed by Finance
- Security requirements must be approved by CISO
- Compliance requirements must be validated by Legal
- Architecture decisions must align with enterprise standards
- All risks must be registered in enterprise risk management system
- Change control process must be followed for all modifications
- Document must be reviewed quarterly or upon significant changes

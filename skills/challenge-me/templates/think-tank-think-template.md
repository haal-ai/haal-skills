## Placeholder Guide

- `{subject}`: The topic or subject area for ideation

- `{timestamp}`: Current timestamp in YYYYMMDD-HHmm format
- `{cycle_count}`: Number of ideation cycles completed
- `{describe_*}`: Detailed descriptive paragraphs
- `{list_*}`: Bulleted or numbered lists of items
- `{findings_from_*}`: Specific findings from each research source type
- `{challenge_title}`: Brief title for each challenge addressed
- `{challenge_description}`: Description of the original concern

- `{evidence_sources}`: Sources supporting the resolution

# OLAF Registry Chain Collection - Functional Details

## Overview
OLAF can now gather skills and prompts from a chain of OLAF registries, making it practical to collect and distribute prompts, skills and other AI instructions from any team.

## Business Impact
- Teams can now share and distribute AI instructions across organizations
- Eliminates silos between different AI implementation teams
- Enables centralized collection of proven AI patterns and skills
- Reduces duplication of effort in AI instruction development

## Implementation Context
- Registry chain architecture allows hierarchical skill discovery
- Teams can maintain their own registries while contributing to shared collections
- Enables both private and public skill sharing models
- Supports decentralized contribution while maintaining quality control

## User Experience Changes
- Users can access skills from multiple team registries
- Simplified discovery of available AI instructions across organization
- Teams can publish their successful patterns for others to use
- Reduced manual effort in finding and implementing AI solutions

## Success Metrics
- Number of registries connected to OLAF instances
- Skills shared and reused across teams
- Reduction in duplicate AI instruction development
- User adoption of shared vs. custom-developed skills
